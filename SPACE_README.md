# The AI Loop

An interactive map of the circular economy of AI: who invests in whom, who buys it back, the share prices, the energy it burns, and the bubble debate. Built from public reporting, current through June 2026.

> **To deploy on Hugging Face:** create a Space with the **Static** SDK, upload `index.html` and `og-image.png`, and use *this file* as the Space's `README.md` (the YAML block above is the Space card). Source, dataset and build tooling live on GitHub.

**Not investment advice.** "Reported" and "letter of intent" figures are intentions or media reports, not confirmed cash.
